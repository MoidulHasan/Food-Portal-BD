About US

Food Portal Ltd. (FPL) is a Bangladeshi Catering & Event Planning Company that serving professionals in the Gulshan area, where affordable but hygienic meals for everyday consumption is a rarity, and so is in many places of rest of Dhaka, where many jobholders struggle to source healthy meals at their desired budget so that they can apply their minds to their actual work at peace. A smaller nuclear family where both partners are working and often with so many other family responsibilities are limiting the ability to consistently prepare their own food at dawn and bring it to the office, not to mention the nuisance of packing/unpacking, organizing, and washing activities associated. Food Portal Ltd. is very concerned to provide hygienic food to their clients.
